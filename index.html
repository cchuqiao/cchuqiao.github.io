Hi! My name is Carol.

I am a Chinese-American student, artist, and software engineer focused on backend development for consumer-facing products. I currently study computer engineering and computer science at the University of Southern California. This summer, I'll be interning at Stripe as a software engineer.

I'm passionate about the intersection between the human experience, tech, and art, and believe that usability and accessibility in a product is just as important as its underlying technical capabilities. I hope to create meaningful experiences and opportunities using my background in the fine arts and computer science.

My interests include oil painting, personal finance, eating good food, and pushing for gender equity in the tech industry. I volunteer with organizations such as the Society of Women Engineers and TechTogether to empower underrepresented groups in engineering.

Currently reading: Development as Freedom by Amartya Sen
Currently learning: latte art, frontend dev (hence this website)
