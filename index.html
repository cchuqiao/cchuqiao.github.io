Hi! My name is Carol.

I am a Chinese-American student, artist, and software engineer focused on backend development for consumer-facing products. I currently study computer engineering and computer science at the University of Southern California. This summer, I'll be interning at Stripe as a software engineer.

I'm passionate about the intersection between experience, technology, and art. I believe that usability and accessibility in a product is just as important as its underlying technical capabilities. I hope to create meaningful experiences and relevant opportunities for the world through my background in the fine arts, music, and computer science.

Outside of academics and career, I enjoy oil painting, personal finance, and exploring good food around the city. I volunteer with organizations such as the Society of Women Engineers and TechTogether Boston to empower underrepresented groups in technology.
