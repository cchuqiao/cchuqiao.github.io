Hi! My name is Carol.

I am a Chinese-American student, artist, and software engineer focused on backend development for consumer-facing products. I currently study computer engineering and computer science at the University of Southern California. This summer, I'll be interning at Stripe as a software engineer.

I'm passionate about the intersection between the human experience, tech, and art, and believe that usability in a product is just as important as its underlying technical capabilities. Throughout my career and onwards, I hope to create meaningful experiences and opportunities using my background in the fine arts and computer science.

My interests include oil painting, personal finance, and eating good food. I volunteer with organizations such as the Society of Women Engineers and TechTogether to empower underrepresented groups in technology.

Currently reading: Development as Freedom by Amartya Sen
Currently learning: latte art, frontend dev (hence this website)
