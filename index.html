Hi! My name is Carol.

I am a software engineer, artist, and student focused on backend engineering for consumer-facing products. I am currently studying computer engineering and computer science at the University of Southern California.

I'm passionate about the intersection between user experience, technology, and art. I believe that usability and accessibilty in product is just as important, if not more so, than its underlying technical capabilities. I hope to bring wonderful experiences and opportunities to the world through tech, through my background in the fine arts, music, and computer science.

Outside of academics and career, I enjoy oil painting, reading, personal finance, exploring good food around the city, and pushing for gender equity in tech. I volunteer with organizations such as Society of Women Engineers @USC and TechTogether Boston to empower underrepresented groups in technology to take that first step in exploring the world of programming.

Currently reading: Development as Freedom by Amartya Sen
